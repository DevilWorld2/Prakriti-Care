Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get personalized recommendations?

Simply create an account and fill out a detailed health questionnaire about your symptoms, dosha type, and health goals. Our AI system will provide personalized herb recommendations.

Are the herbs safe for everyone?

While Ayurvedic herbs are naturally-derived, they're not suitable for everyone. Always consult with a healthcare professional, especially if pregnant, nursing, or taking medications.

What is my dosha?

Your dosha (Vata, Pitta, or Kapha) is your unique constitutional type in Ayurveda. Take our dosha assessment quiz to discover your primary dosha and how to balance it.

Can I use PrakritiCare alongside modern medicine?

PrakritiCare complements modern medicine but doesn't replace it. Always inform your doctor about herbs you're using and maintain professional medical care for serious conditions.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on the condition and individual constitution. Some benefits appear within days, while chronic conditions may require consistent use for weeks or months.

Do you offer shipping worldwide?

We currently ship to India and selected countries. Contact us to check availability in your location.
