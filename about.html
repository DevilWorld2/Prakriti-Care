About PrakritiCare

Bridging Ancient Wisdom with Modern Technology

Our Mission

PrakritiCare is dedicated to making Ayurvedic healthcare accessible and scientifically validated. We combine classical Ayurvedic texts with modern data-driven approaches to provide intelligent drug recommendations tailored to individual health profiles.

What We Do

Our intelligent system analyzes health conditions, dosha imbalances, and classical Ayurvedic references to recommend appropriate herbal formulations. We believe in:

  • Authenticity: Following proven Ayurvedic principles from trusted texts
  • Safety: Recommending natural, non-toxic herbal solutions
  • Personalization: Tailored recommendations based on individual constitution
  • Holistic Wellness: Addressing root causes, not just symptoms

Why Ayurveda?

Ayurveda, the "Science of Life," is one of the world's oldest medical systems with over 5,000 years of documented practice. It emphasizes:

  • Balance of three doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha)
  • Prevention through lifestyle and nutrition
  • Natural healing with herbs and botanicals
  • Treating the whole person, not isolated symptoms

Our Technology

PrakritiCare uses advanced algorithms and machine learning to match symptoms, diseases, and constitutional types with the most appropriate Ayurvedic herbs and formulations from classical texts. This ensures recommendations are both traditional and scientifically modern.
